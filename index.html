Welcome to Hebron Counseling:

House of Refuge for Healing and Growth

At Hebron Counseling, we are dedicated to providing compassionate, faith-based counseling and therapy to help individuals, couples, and families navigate life’s challenges. Our approach combines professional therapy with biblical principles, offering a safe and supportive space for healing, growth, and restoration. Inspired by the biblical city of Hebron—a city of refuge—we are here to walk alongside you in your journey towards hope, transformation, and a brighter future.

Why Choose Hebron Counseling?

House of Refuge for Healing and Growth

  • Biblically Grounded: Our counselors are committed to using scripture and Christian values as the foundation for therapy, helping you find peace and healing in God’s Word.
  • Experienced and Compassionate Team: Our licensed counselors are experienced in grief, marriage, family dynamics, and child development, and are passionate about helping others find hope and restoration.
  • Safe and Supportive Environment: Just as the city of Hebron was a refuge, our counseling space is a place where you can feel safe, supported, and understood as you work through life’s difficulties.
  • Personalized Care: We tailor each counseling session to meet your unique needs, offering practical tools and spiritual encouragement to help you achieve lasting change.

Our Approach

Faith-Based,Compassionate Care

At Hebron Counseling, we believe that true healing comes from a holistic approach that nurtures the mind, body, and spirit. Loss is never easy, and navigating grief can feel overwhelming. At Hebron Counseling, we offer specialized grief counseling to help you process and heal from the pain of loss. Whether you’ve lost a loved one, a relationship, or even a dream, our experienced counselors are here to guide you with compassion, empathy, and biblical wisdom. We believe that while grief is a natural part of life, God provides a path to healing, peace, and restoration.
